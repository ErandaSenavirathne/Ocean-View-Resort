Choose a Payment Method

Select Cash or Card before finalizing — each follows a different flow.

4

The two options trigger different confirmation flows.

1
Cash is Selected by Default
When the bill page loads, 💵 Cash Payment is already active (blue border). If the guest is paying cash, you don't need to change anything.
2
Switch to Card if Needed
Click the 💳 Card Payment button to switch. The form action changes and clicking Confirm will open the card authorization screen instead.
3
Only One Can Be Active
The active method is highlighted with a blue border. Clicking the other option automatically deactivates the previous one.
4
Cannot Change After Confirming
Once you click Confirm & Finalize the payment method is locked into the booking record. Make sure the correct method is selected first.
5

Completing a Cash Check-Out

The simplest path — confirm and the system finalizes everything instantly.

5
Cash Payment Flow

💵 Cash selected → Confirm & Finalize → receipt page.

1
Collect Cash from Guest
Collect the Total Payable amount shown in the bill card from the guest before clicking confirm.
2
Ensure 💵 Cash is Active
Confirm the Cash Payment button has the blue border (active state). It is selected by default but verify it hasn't been accidentally changed.
3
Click "Confirm & Finalize"
Submit the form. The system marks the reservation as PAID, releases the room(s) to AVAILABLE status, and redirects to the receipt page.
4
Receipt Page Opens
The official receipt is generated automatically. Print it for the guest using the Print Receipt button (covered in Step 7).
✅
Rooms Released Automatically After confirming, the rooms are immediately marked as AVAILABLE in the system — no manual update needed. They will appear free in the 📋 Room Status screen right away.

Computer Generated Receipt

1
Receipt Loads Automatically
After confirming payment (cash or card), the system generates the receipt page immediately with all itemized charges pulled from the database.
2
Click "Print Receipt"
Click the Print Receipt button at the bottom of the page. The browser's print dialog opens — select the connected printer and print.
3
Hand Receipt to Guest
Give the printed receipt to the guest. The receipt shows: booking ID, guest name & NIC, room(s), nights, all food orders, and the total amount paid.
4
Click "Close" to Return
After printing, click the Close button to return to the dashboard. The check-out is now fully complete.

Quick Reference
?
Common Scenarios & What To Do
?
Guest NIC search shows no result
Verify the NIC is correct. Also check 📅 View Bookings — the reservation might already be PAID (already checked out) or CANCELLED.
?
Food total is wrong or missing
Food charges are pulled automatically from orders placed via 🍽️ Food Ordering. If items are missing, add them there before processing check-out.
?
Guest checking out early
The system detects early check-out automatically and shows the EARLY badge. The bill is recalculated for actual nights — no manual adjustment needed.
?
Receipt page won't load
If the receipt page fails, go to 📅 View Bookings, find the booking (should show PAID), and use your browser's back button carefully — do not re-submit.
