1
Filter by Category
Click Breakfast, Lunch, or Dinner tabs to filter items. Use All Items to see the full menu.
2
Click an Item to Add
Tap any food card to add it to the cart. Tapping the same item again increases the quantity by 1.
3
Cart Updates Instantly
The right panel shows each item, quantity, and subtotal. The grand total at the bottom updates automatically.
4
Remove an Item
Click the 🗑️ icon next to any cart item to remove it entirely. To clear everything, use Clear Selection.
🍽️
Multiple Items You can order across different categories in a single session — for example, add breakfast items and drinks together. All items will be billed in one order.
4

Review the Order Cart

Check all items and total before adding to the room bill.

4
Verify the Order Summary

Confirm items, quantities, and the final total with the guest.

1
Check Each Line Item
Verify every item name and quantity matches what the guest ordered. Use 🗑️ to remove anything incorrect.
2
Confirm the Grand Total
Show the total to the guest so they know the amount being added to their room bill before you confirm.
3
Need to Start Over?
Click Clear Selection — a confirmation popup will appear. Confirm to empty the entire cart and start fresh.
4
Ready? Proceed to Confirm
Once everything looks correct, click the green Add to Room Bill ✓ button to finalize the order.
🗑️
Clear Selection?
Remove all items from the current order?
⚠️
Validation Checks The system will block submission with a popup if: (1) no guest is selected, or (2) the cart is empty. Make sure both are filled before clicking Add to Room Bill.
5

Confirm & Submit the Order

The order is sent to the kitchen and charges are added to the guest's room bill.

5
Order Placed Successfully

A confirmation popup confirms the order was processed.

✔
Order Placed!
The food items have been successfully added to the room bill.
1
Success Popup Appears
A green "Order Placed!" popup confirms the food items were added to the guest's room bill automatically.
2
Click "New Order"
Returns you to the food ordering screen with a fresh empty cart — ready to take another order immediately.
3
Click "Dashboard"
Returns you to the main staff dashboard if no more food orders need to be placed right now.
4
If an Error Appears
A red error popup means the order could not be saved. Click New Order to retry — do not submit twice from the same screen.
✖
System Error
Could not process the order. Please try again.
✅
Order Complete! The food charges are now part of the guest's room bill. They will appear in the total amount due when staff processes their check-out via 🚪 Process Check-Out.
Quick Reference
?
Common Scenarios & What To Do
?
Guest not appearing in search
Only active (BOOKED) reservations appear. Check their booking status in 📅 View Bookings on the dashboard.
?
Wrong guest was selected
Click the Change button on the green confirmation banner to open the search again without losing your cart items.
?
Added wrong quantity of an item
Remove the item using the 🗑️ icon, then re-add it the correct number of times by clicking its card.
?
Want to check total before confirming
The running total is always visible at the bottom of the cart panel on the right. It updates instantly as items are added or removed.
