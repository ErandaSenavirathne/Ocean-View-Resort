Quick Reference
Common Scenarios & What To Do
?
Guest has no prior record
Register first via 👤 Register Guest, then return to dashboard and use 🔑 New Reservation to book.
?
Returning guest wants to book
Skip registration. Go directly to 🔑 New Reservation, search by NIC, and proceed through the 3-step wizard.
?
No rooms available for dates
Go back to Step 1 of the wizard and choose different check-in / check-out dates. Use 📋 Room Status to plan ahead.
?
Need to view all bookings
Click 📅 View Bookings from the dashboard to see and filter the complete reservation list.
