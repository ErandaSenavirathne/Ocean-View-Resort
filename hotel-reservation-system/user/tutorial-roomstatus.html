★

Understanding Room Statuses

What each status indicator means and what action to take.

●
Status Indicator Reference

Green = free, Orange = occupied.

✅
● AVAILABLE
The room has no active booking for the current date (or the filtered date range). It is ready to be reserved for a new guest.
🛏️
● OCCUPIED
A guest is currently staying in this room, or the room has an active booking covering the filtered date range. It cannot be booked until the guest checks out.
⚠️
Important A room showing AVAILABLE in the live view (no date filter) could still be reserved for future dates. Always use the date filter to confirm availability for specific check-in and check-out dates before creating a reservation.

Filter Availability by Date Range

Check which rooms are free for a specific check-in and check-out period.

3
Use the Date Filter Box

Select From and To dates, then click Check Availability.

1
Click the "From" Date Field
Select the guest's intended check-in date. Past dates are automatically blocked — only today and future dates can be selected.
2
Click the "To" Date Field
Select the intended check-out date. This must be after the From date — the system will enforce this automatically.
3
Click "Check Availability"
The table refreshes to show only rooms whose status covers the selected date range. OCCUPIED rooms have a booking overlapping those dates.
4
Read the Results
A yellow banner confirms the active filter period. Rooms showing AVAILABLE can be safely booked for those dates.

4

When No Rooms Are Available

What to do if all rooms are occupied for the selected dates.

!
All Rooms Fully Occupied

The table shows an empty state when no rooms match the criteria.

Showing room status for the period: 2025-12-24 — 2025-12-27
Room No.CategoryPrice / NightLive Status
🏨
No rooms match your search criteria.
1
Try Adjusting the Dates
Click Reset and enter a different date range — perhaps shift the check-in or check-out by one or two days to find an opening.
2
Check a Shorter Stay
A shorter stay period may have available rooms. Try reducing the date range to identify which specific days are fully booked.
3
Inform the Guest
Let the guest know there are no rooms for their requested dates and offer alternative dates or a waitlist if the resort supports it.
4
Check View Bookings
Go to 📅 View Bookings to see which rooms have check-outs scheduled — one may free up on the date the guest needs.

Quick Reference
?
Common Questions & Scenarios
?
Room shows AVAILABLE but can't be booked
The live view shows today's status. Run the date filter for the guest's exact dates — it may already be reserved for that period.
?
How do I see who's in an OCCUPIED room?
Go to 📅 View Bookings on the dashboard and search or filter by room number to see the current guest's details.
?
Prices shown here vs. reservation wizard differ
The prices here are the base nightly rates. The reservation bill may be different after automatic discounts (long stay / multi-room) are applied.
?
Filter shows dates in the past
The system blocks past dates in the date picker. If you need to check historical occupancy, use 📅 View Bookings and filter by date range there.
